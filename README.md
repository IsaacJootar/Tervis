# **Cureva Health Platform**
### AI-Powered Smart Data Automation for Health Facilities

**Cureva** is an AI-powered, multi-tenant digital health platform designed for primary and secondary healthcare facilities.  
It replaces manual record-keeping systems with intelligent, structured, and automated health data management.

Built for hospitals, maternity centers, clinics, and health administrators, Cureva enables real-time patient data capture, analytics, and decision support.

---

## 🚀 Core Vision
To provide a unified digital platform that enables healthcare facilities to:
- Capture accurate patient data  
- Automate registers and reports  
- Improve maternal and general healthcare outcomes  
- Enable AI-assisted decision making  
- Centralize facility-level and national health data  

---

## 🧠 Key Features

### 🏥 Multi-Facility & Multi-Tenant
- Supports multiple hospitals and health centers  
- Role-based access per facility  
- Centralized oversight with local control  

### 👩‍⚕️ Patient & Clinical Management
- Digital patient registration  
- Visit and consultation records  
- ANC & maternal health tracking  
- Immunization and treatment records  
- Lab and test documentation  

### 📊 Smart Health Data & Analytics
- Real-time dashboards  
- Automated registers and reports  
- Facility performance tracking  
- Health outcome monitoring  

### 🤖 AI-Powered Tools
- Smart clinical decision support  
- Automated risk detection  
- Intelligent data insights  

### 📁 Digital Register Automation
Replaces manual registers such as:
- ANC Register  
- Delivery Register  
- Immunization Register  
- Attendance & Visit Logs  
- Facility Reports  

---

## 🔐 Security & Access Control
- Role-based authentication  
- Facility-level data isolation  
- Secure medical record handling  
- Audit logs & activity tracking  

---

## 🏗️ Technology Stack
Cureva is built using modern, scalable technologies:

- **Laravel** — Backend framework  
- **Livewire** — Dynamic frontend  
- **Tailwind CSS** — UI styling  
- **Alpine.js** — Lightweight interactivity  
- **MySQL** — Database  
- **AI integrations** — Smart automation & insights
